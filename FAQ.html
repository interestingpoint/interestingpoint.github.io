FAQ

Posting Lifespan

Postings are live on the site for different amounts of time depending on the type of posting. Paid postings are live on the site for 30 days. Publishing your posting is a one time charge. Craigslist does not have any automated renewal fees, subscription fees, or additional charges. It is not possible to move an existing paid posting to the top of the listings. A new posting or repost must be created and purchased to be listed at the top of "by date" search results. Free postings are live on the site for between 7 and 45 days. Event postings are live on the site depending on the information provided in the posting. All postings move down the listings as new postings are published.

When should I pay for my post?

Only some categories and geographic areas are paid postings. If you are making a paid posting, you will be notified of the price when you begin the posting. After you have created the posting (including the map and images) you will be prompted to confirm the posting, then provide payment information.

Why is my post on hold?

Postings may be held temporarily for manual review by craigslist staff. New postings held for review will generally be top-posted upon approval. Please reload the currently held posting to check on its status.
